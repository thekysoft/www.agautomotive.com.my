BUSINESS MODEL – DEALERSHIP STRATEGY

We are launching a modern used car dealership that combines multiple income streams for stronger returns and flexibility. Here's how we're proceeding:

1. Lease-to-Own Program

We acquire auction cars at 20–30% below market value. These units will be restored and offered under a lease-to-own structure:

  • • Customers pay monthly for 1–5 years
  • • Ownership is transferred after full payment
  • • This model targets customers who may not qualify for bank loans

2. Customized New Car Sales

We offer customization for new car models (e.g. body kits, wraps, performance upgrades) and resell to a premium niche:

  • • Ideal for customers seeking unique or personalized vehicles
  • • Higher margins compared to standard used car sales

3. In-House Credit Program

We finance customers directly and charge interest on monthly installments. This reduces reliance on external credit providers and increases dealership profit:

  • • Competitive but profitable interest rates
  • • Credit checks managed in-house
  • • Payments collected monthly (with options for auto-debit)

RENTAL FLEET EXPANSION

To strengthen recurring income, we are expanding our rental operations:

  • • Daily Rentals: Using reliable auction cars (Yaris, Vios, BMW, etc.)
  • • Event & Wedding Cars: Fair Lady, Alphard, BMW 5 Series, etc.
  • • Marketing Partnership: We'll collaborate with a national rental marketing platform to increase visibility and bookings

This model delivers:

  • • Recurring cash flow from rentals
  • • Reduced CAPEX due to discounted car sourcing
  • • Synergy with detailing and repainting units (self-maintenance)
